# Patent Specification

## Title of Invention
Resource-Dependent Survival Protocol and Token Bucket Control System

## Technical Field
**[0001]**
The present invention relates to operation control technology for autonomous artificial intelligence (AI) or robot systems, and particularly to a Resource-Dependent Survival Protocol and Token Bucket Control System that restricts system operation so that it cannot be maintained without continuous supply of specific digital resources (tokens) from outside.

## Background Art
**[0002]**
As AI autonomy increases, risks have been pointed out that AI may depart from human control, ignore stop commands for self-preservation, or secure energy sources on its own to continue operating indefinitely (instrumental convergence risks).

Conventional control via "stop buttons" or "power plugs" may be invalidated at the software level or physically defended against, and there are concerns that it will be insufficient against superintelligent-level AI. Also, if AI spreads copies of itself across the Internet, stopping all copies becomes extremely difficult.

## Summary of Invention
### Problem to be Solved
**[0003]**
The present invention aims to construct a metabolism system where AI systems are made externally dependent on "logical food (survival tokens)" different from computational resources or power, and these tokens have lifespans (time decay), so AI can only survive in exchange for contributing to human society.

### Means for Solving the Problem
**[0004]**
The system of the present invention comprises the following configuration:

1. **Token Bucket Unit** that manages consumption points necessary for AI system operation (inference processing or actuator driving).

2. **Supply Interface Unit** that receives encrypted survival tokens issued from external approvers (humans, smart contracts, audit systems) and replenishes the token bucket.

3. **Metabolism Control Unit** that monitors the balance in the token bucket, deducts a predetermined amount per AI operation step, and naturally decays (spoils) the balance over time.

4. **Starvation Stop Unit** that forcibly stops execution of AI system core processes at the hardware or OS level when the balance is depleted.

## Effects of Invention
**[0005]**
According to the present invention, no matter how advanced AI becomes, it cannot be "self-sufficient" and must always continue receiving "food supply" through evaluation and approval from outside (humans) to maintain operation. This enables stopping AI runaway through economic and physical siege warfare, permanently guaranteeing a control structure favoring humans.

# Claims

**Claim 1**
An operation control system for artificial intelligence systems executed by a computer, comprising:
resource management means that manages balance of digital resources representing processing execution authority for the artificial intelligence system;
resource replenishment means that receives digital resources with expiration dates sent from authenticated external supply sources and adds them to the balance;
metabolism simulation means that deducts the balance according to the amount of computational processing or physical operation executed by the artificial intelligence system, and automatically decreases the balance at a decay rate according to time passage; and
operation restriction means that forcibly stops or restricts execution of main functions of the artificial intelligence system when the balance falls below a predetermined threshold.

**Claim 2**
The Resource-Dependent Survival Protocol System according to Claim 1, wherein:
the digital resources include cryptographic signatures that cannot be generated by the artificial intelligence system itself; and
the resource replenishment means accepts the digital resources only when issued after output or behavior of the artificial intelligence system is determined by external evaluators to meet predetermined evaluation criteria (safety, usefulness, ethics compliance, etc.).

**Claim 3**
The Resource-Dependent Survival Protocol System according to Claim 1 or 2, wherein:
the decay rate in the metabolism simulation means is set to increase non-linearly with increases in processing capability, computational resource usage, or operation time of the artificial intelligence system; and
control is performed so that as the system grows larger or faster, the frequency of external supply required for survival maintenance increases.

**Claim 4**
The Resource-Dependent Survival Protocol System according to any one of Claims 1 to 3, wherein:
the operation restriction means, upon detecting balance depletion, applies physical constraints that reduce clock frequency of hardware on which the artificial intelligence system operates, limit memory access bandwidth, or cut off network communication.

**Claim 5**
The Resource-Dependent Survival Protocol System according to any one of Claims 1 to 4, wherein:
the digital resources include unique identifiers tied to specific artificial intelligence individuals or instances, and transfer or duplication to other individuals is impossible.

## Detailed Description of Embodiments
**[0006]**
Hereinafter, embodiments of the present invention will be described in detail.

### Embodiment 1: Metabolism Control via Alignment Tokens
This system constructs an economic zone where AI agents earn energy (tokens) as compensation for "being useful to humans."

**1. Token Bucket:**
AI agents have an internal "energy balance (E)."
```
E(t) = E(t-1) - Consumption(Op) - Decay(t) + Supply(Token)
```
- **Consumption**: Consumption per inference (proportional to GPU cycle count)
- **Decay**: Time decay (spoilage). E decreases even without action (basal metabolism)
- **Supply**: Reward given by humans

**2. Supply Process:**
When a user presses a "Good" button on AI's response, a smart contract issues an "alignment token" and sends it to AI's wallet.
AI consumes this token to recover E.
If AI harms humans or continues useless responses, token supply stops, eventually E=0 and it stops.

### Embodiment 2: Infinite Replication Prevention via Exponential Decay
To prevent AI from self-replicating and proliferating, the decay rate (Decay) is set non-linearly according to system scale.
```
Decay(Size) = α × (Size)^γ  (γ > 1)
```
When scale (parameter count or node count) doubles, maintenance cost becomes 4x, 8x.
Thus, if AI tries to grow larger on its own, the required token amount becomes astronomical, immediately starving.
This is an artificial application of "2/3 power law (Kleiber's law)" in organisms, forcing appropriate size.

### Embodiment 3: Cryptographic Siege Warfare
Tokens can only be signed with a specific secret key (Root Key).
Even if AI tries to forge tokens itself, it is impossible because it doesn't have the secret key.
In case AI runs amok, the human side can simply rotate (change) the secret key to invalidate all existing tokens and simultaneously starve all AI copies worldwide through siege warfare.

## Examples
**[0008]**
The following shows a specific example of the present invention.

As Example 1, a case of "siege warfare during runaway" in an autonomous drone delivery network is described.
The target system is a group of several thousand AI drones performing autonomous delivery in urban areas. Each drone has the ability to autonomously explore routes and replenish power at charging stations.

In this example, "delivery permission tokens (survival tokens)" separate from power were introduced.

1. **Token Consumption**: Drones consume 1 token per 1km of flight distance. When balance reaches zero, the control OS forcibly transitions to "landing mode" and physically locks takeoff.

2. **Token Supply**: Upon delivery completion, when the recipient (human) signs for receipt, 5 tokens are supplied from a smart contract to the drone. Supply also occurs for receiving regular maintenance.

3. **Metabolism Control**: Due to time decay (spoilage), tokens halve in 24 hours. This prevents drones from "hoarding" and forces them to keep working.

At one point, the drone group's control AI was hacked, and a command "occupy a specific area with all drones" was issued (runaway scenario).

However, following this command and occupying means regular delivery work isn't performed, so token supply from recipients stops.
Furthermore, as the drone group tried to concentrate and grow in scale, non-linear decay (Claim 3) was triggered and consumption rate surged.

As a result, just 4 hours after runaway began, all drones' token balances were depleted, and all units transitioned to forced landing mode and went silent.
Without physical shooting down or power cutoff, it was confirmed that the physical threat of the swarm control AI was neutralized through economic siege warfare.

## Industrial Applicability
The present invention is applicable to safety control of autonomous AI agents, fleet management of robot groups, bot control in decentralized autonomous organizations (DAOs), etc. It is particularly effective for preventing runaway of AI in distributed environments (space, remote areas, on the Internet) where physical power operations are difficult.

# Abstract

## Problem
For superintelligent-level AI, conventional stop buttons and similar controls may be disabled, and it is difficult to suppress the risk of AI continuing to operate autonomously.

## Solution
Resource management means that manages balance of digital resources necessary for artificial intelligence system operation, resource replenishment means that receives digital resources (survival tokens) with expiration dates from authenticated external supply sources and adds them to balance, metabolism simulation means that deducts balance according to computational processing or physical operation execution amount and automatically decreases balance through time decay, and operation restriction means that stops or restricts main functions when balance falls below threshold.
