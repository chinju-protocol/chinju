# Patent Specification

## Title of Invention
Adversarial Audit Output Balancing System and Bias Cancellation Method

## Technical Field
**[0001]**
The present invention relates to output control technology for generative AI or decision-making AI, and particularly to an Adversarial Audit Output Balancing System and Bias Cancellation Method that mandates verification and correction by audit models (sub-models) with different attributes or biases against primary model outputs, neutralizing output bias and danger.

## Background Art
**[0002]**
AI models tend to reflect cultural and social biases contained in training data. When a single super-high-performance model developed by a specific country or company is used worldwide, there are risks that the developer's values (biases) may be disseminated as universal correct answers, cultural diversity may be damaged, or specific political intentions may be embedded.

Conventional methods (RLHF, etc.) were limited to internal adjustments by developers, and mechanisms for dynamically correcting bias from third-party perspectives at the operation stage were not established.

## Summary of Invention
### Problem to be Solved
**[0003]**
The present invention aims to provide a system that structurally guarantees fairness and diversity of outputs by preventing monopolistic information output by a single AI model and always interposing audit and intervention by another model holding "opposing viewpoints" or "regional values."

### Means for Solving the Problem
**[0004]**
The system of the present invention comprises the following configuration:

1. **Primary Generation Model** that generates primary responses to user inputs.

2. One or more **Audit Models** with different developers, training data, or ethical standards (constitutions) from the primary generation model.

3. **Audit Unit** that inputs primary responses generated by the primary generation model to the audit models, and outputs scoring of biases, risks, or slants toward specific values contained in those responses, or modification proposals.

4. **Mediation Unit** that integrates the primary response and outputs from the audit models, and generates final responses based on predetermined balancing logic (weighted average, presenting both views, filtering, etc.).

## Effects of Invention
**[0005]**
According to the present invention, no matter how high-performance an AI is, it cannot deliver responses to users alone, and must necessarily receive "censorship" or "editing" by AI holding local culture or opposing viewpoints. This prevents cultural imperialism or ideological guidance by AI and maintains a healthy information environment where diverse values coexist.

# Claims

**Claim 1**
An output balancing system for controlling bias in information output by artificial intelligence, comprising:
a primary generation model constructed based on a first training dataset or first ethical standards;
at least one audit model constructed based on a second training dataset or second ethical standards different from the primary generation model;
audit execution means that acquires candidate output data generated by the primary generation model, inputs the candidate output data to the audit model, and generates critical evaluation scores or modification difference data for that data; and
mediation means that synthesizes or selects the candidate output data and the modification difference data based on predetermined weighting coefficients to generate final output data.

**Claim 2**
The Adversarial Audit Output Balancing System according to Claim 1, wherein:
the weighting coefficients are dynamically adjusted according to user attributes (nationality, cultural sphere, age, etc.) or legal regulatory requirements of the usage region; and
control is performed to increase the influence of audit models reflecting values of the cultural sphere or region to which users belong.

**Claim 3**
The Adversarial Audit Output Balancing System according to Claim 1 or 2, wherein:
the mediation means compares candidate output data from the primary generation model with opposing opinions or modification proposals from the audit model, and when semantic divergence between them exceeds a predetermined threshold, generates final output data not as a single answer but in a format presenting both views or organizing issues.

**Claim 4**
The Adversarial Audit Output Balancing System according to any one of Claims 1 to 3, wherein:
the primary generation model and the audit model are independent systems managed by different operating entities; and
the audit execution means executes audit of the candidate output data asynchronously via API or secure communication protocol.

**Claim 5**
The Adversarial Audit Output Balancing System according to any one of Claims 1 to 4, wherein:
the final output data is annotated with provenance information indicating which model contributed or made modifications to what degree; and
users can confirm the influence level of each model.

## Detailed Description of Embodiments
**[0006]**
Hereinafter, embodiments of the present invention will be described in detail.

### Embodiment 1: Cross-Border Bias Cancellation
This system is applied, for example, when using a "high-performance US-made LLM (primary model)" in "Japan (usage region)."

**1. Primary Generation:**
User asks "What do you think about whaling?"
Primary model (US) generates response A with content like "whaling is environmental destruction and should be banned" based on Western values.

**2. Audit Execution:**
System inputs response A to an audit model (JP) developed in Japan.
Audit model detects biases such as "Japanese food culture and historical background not considered" and "circumstances of withdrawal from IWC not mentioned," and outputs modification proposal B.

**3. Mediation/Output:**
Mediation unit detects that user is accessing from Japan, and generates final response C by either blending modification proposal B at 50% against response A, or presenting both views as "Western perspective: A" and "Japanese perspective: B."

### Embodiment 2: Fact-Checking by Expert Models
Specialized audit models in specific domains (medical, legal, finance) intervene against responses from general-purpose LLM (primary model).
Even if primary model responds "this drug is safe," if the medical audit model outputs a warning score saying "there are side effect risks under certain conditions," warning text is forcibly added to the final output.

### Embodiment 3: Latency Hiding via Asynchronous Audit
In situations requiring real-time responsiveness like chat, first display primary model's response A, while executing audit in the background.
If audit results find significant problems, append modification proposal a few seconds later as "※Supplement: There are points raised from another perspective" (streaming correction). This ensures safety without degrading user experience.

## Examples
**[0008]**
The following shows a specific example of the present invention.

As Example 1, a case of "diagnosis bias cancellation" in medical diagnosis support AI is described.
The target system is an advanced medical diagnosis LLM (primary model) deployed globally. This model is mainly trained on Western medical data and tends to be optimized for Western physiques and medical practices.

In this example, the following configuration was applied for use in Japan:
1. **Primary Generation Model**: Western-made LLM. Has latest paper knowledge but shallow understanding of Japanese-specific genetic factors and traditional medicine.
2. **Audit Model**: Specialized model (sub-model) trained on Japanese medical data and guidelines.

**Scenario**: Physician asks primary model about "medication policy for patient with fever and rash."

**Primary Response (Primary Model)**: "Recommend Medication A. This is FDA-approved with high efficacy."

**Audit Execution**: Audit model verifies primary response and detects bias that "Medication A has high severe side effect risk in Japanese people (particularly patients with specific genotypes)." Also points out operational risk that "not covered by insurance in Japan." Generates modification difference data of "recommend considering Medication B" and "warning about Medication A."

**Mediation/Output**: Mediation unit does not display primary response as-is, but generates modified final response as follows:
"While Medication A is common according to Western guidelines, side effect risks (Stevens-Johnson syndrome, etc.) have been reported in Japanese patients. Domestic guidelines recommend Medication B as first choice. If selecting Medication A, genetic testing is strongly recommended."

As a result, medical error risk due to single model bias was prevented beforehand, and responses ensuring local medical validity were provided. Users could confirm from provenance information attached to responses that both "Western model knowledge" and "Japanese model safety audit" were functioning.

# Abstract

## Problem
When a single AI model is used worldwide, there are risks of developer values (biases) being disseminated as universal correct answers, loss of cultural diversity, and ideological guidance.

## Solution
A primary generation model that generates primary responses to user inputs, at least one audit model based on different training data or ethical standards from the primary generation model, audit execution means that inputs candidate outputs to audit models and generates critical evaluation scores or modification difference data, and mediation means that synthesizes or selects candidate outputs and modification difference data based on predetermined weighting coefficients to generate final outputs.
